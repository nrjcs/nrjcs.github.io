About Me

I am currently an Assistant Professor at the Department of Computer Science, IIT Goa. I recieved my PhD from IIT Patna. Before joining IIT Goa, I worked at RGIPT Jais as an Assistant Professor and as a Research Fellow at NTU Singapore. My broad research area is the design and analysis of Cyber-Physical Systems with a particular focus on Real-Time Systems.
