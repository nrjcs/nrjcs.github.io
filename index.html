I am currently an Assistant Professor in the School of Mathematics and Computer Science (CSE) at IIT Goa. I received my PhD from IIT Patna. Prior to joining IIT Goa, I worked as an Assistant Professor at RGIPT Jais and as a Research Fellow at NTU Singapore. My broad research interests lie in the design and analysis of Cyber-Physical Systems, with a particular focus on Real-Time Systems.
